Next Step for Trello

This extension extends Trello.com.

It displays your checklist items directly on any Trello board. You can check off the ones you completed, without having to open any card.

Usage instructions

  1. Open any of your Trello boards.
  2. Checklist items should be displayed on the front of cards that contain any.
  3. Still from the board view, click on an item to mark it as completed.
  4. To adjust your preferences, click on the Next Steps button from your board's toolbar.
